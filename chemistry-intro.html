Tips for you and your students
 
Create or Connect your ORCID iD!
 
ORCID is a unique identifier that disambiguates authors with similar names. It is personal to you and not tied to any institution or database. All academic staff are encouraged to link ORCIDs to their University IDs. See our ORCID Library Guide for more information on how to connect!
Strategies for Research & Writing
 
These research and writing strategies on our WI+RE website are now organized into themed collections from Getting Started and Finding and citing sources to Writing literature reviews.
Publish in eScholarship
 
eScholarship is the University of California's institutional repository. Faculty, researchers, and students can post individual academic works. eScholarship also allows authors to comply with the UC Open Access policy and public policy mandates.
