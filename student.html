Welcome Bruins!
The WI+RE team is here to make sure that you are prepared and well-equipped to tackle all of your writing and research endeavors for the 2017-2018 school year.

WI+RE (Writing Instruction + Research Education) is a UCLA community of students, instructors, and academic support staff that provides online modules for research, writing, and information literacy skills. Our resources are designed to empower students with practical skills and resources necessary to excel at UCLA and beyond.

WI+RE modules can be easily accessed through our website, Youtube channel, and CCLE. If you have a question about research, or if you are looking for help with your writing and research skills, WI+RE is the perfect place to start!
