What's WI+RE?

WI+RE (Writing Instruction + Research Education) is a growing campus community of learners and teachers interested in designing new approaches to teaching and learning challenges. Starting this spring, WI+RE will send out community update emails to share our events, gather ideas and insights, and get the word out about our new creations.

To keep in touch with WI+RE, visit our community sign-up page and add your email address.

If you know someone that would be interested, please share this opportunity!
