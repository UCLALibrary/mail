Curious about participating in design jams to explore teaching and learning challenges?

Sign-up for WI+RE Community Updates
 
 
 
WI+RE (Writing Instruction + Research Education) is a growing campus community of learners and teachers who get together to design new approaches to teaching and learning challenges.
 
Starting this spring, WI+RE will send out community update emails to share our events, gather ideas and insights, and get the word out about our new creations.
 
To keep in touch with WI+RE, visit our community sign-up page and add your email address.
 
If you know someone that would be interested, please share this opportunity!
 
Questions? Let us know what is on your mind!
